How We Do It

We ensure that our security personnel are hired after a thorough investigation, verification and background checks. A majority of our hired personnel are ex-servicemen from the Indian Army, BSF, CRPF, SPG and even NSG, with more than 15-25 years of experience in their respective fields. We ensure to check the background of the applicant at the first step. We have a well-established recruitment program that helps us to hire only the best for our jobs. And those who are selected are trained, tested and given short-term assignments first to check their compatibility and then provided with stringent tasks after competition of their on-job training.
